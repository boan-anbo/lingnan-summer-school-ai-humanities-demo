repository: Digital Commons @ Lingnan University

location: page 12

nearby wording:

> In June of 1900 ... the school was moved to Macao ... where it remained until ... 1904.

human note:

> This supports Macao as an important temporary site in the early history of the institution. It does not by itself prove that Lingnan was simply founded in Macao.

possible reading:

The source is close to the events and describes a move to Macao in 1900, followed by a move back to Canton in 1904. This complicates the simple claim that Lingnan began in either only Macau or only Guangzhou.

uncertainty / next check:

Compare this with earlier institutional records and later retrospective accounts, including `Fifty Years Ago at Lingnan`.
