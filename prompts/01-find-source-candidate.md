# Scene 1 Prompt: Find One Source Candidate

Use the skill in `skills/finding-source-candidates/SKILL.md`.

Classroom-safe mode:

Do not browse or download during the demo unless the instructor explicitly asks you to. Instead, read the prepared candidate record in:

`sources/lingnan-founding-source-candidate.md`

Then explain briefly that this is the kind of result the source-finding workflow should produce.

Question:

> Did Lingnan begin or get founded in Macau around 1899, or in Canton/Guangzhou?

Task:

Return exactly one credible source candidate from the prepared record.

Rules:

- Do not decide the final answer.
- Prefer original or published materials: PDFs, scans, records, repository files, books, articles, or reports.
- Treat search results, AI summaries, and overview pages as leads to files, citations, titles, authors, dates, and variant names.
- Do not stop at a lead when it points to an underlying source.
- For this demo, use the local file already saved in `sources/`.
- Return one compact record with title, owner, date, URL, source type, local path, why it is credible, and what still needs to be checked.
